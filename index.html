ABOUT

I have been in crypto since late 2019 and have seen and experienced much of the crypto industries ups and downs. Obsession has led me to be very knowledgeable of the overall landscape of crypto through countless wins and losses.

Shekels is a name I never expected to carry such meaning in the work I do. When I made the account in 2024 I had no idea what a shekel even was. I thought it was just an ancient coin that sounded funny when people said it on crypto twitter.

Little did I know the implications and associations of what this name represented. Now I see it as a responsibility to help build a better future for crypto and ultimately the world.
